Qualitative Feedback

  • "The one-click draft generation saved me hours of formatting work." - P2
  • "I finally enjoyed group work for the first time in my degree!" - P1
  • "The AI suggestions felt like having a smart research partner." - P3
  • "The interface is intuitive and reduced my research anxiety." - P4

Identified Issues & Improvements

While overall feedback was positive, we identified several areas for improvement:

  1. The "Invite Collaborator" button was not easily discoverable (addressed by moving it to the main toolbar)
  2. Some users wanted the option to disable celebratory animations (added in settings)
  3. Mobile experience needed optimization for smaller screens (implemented responsive design improvements)
